"Bits and Paradoxes"

I maintain a weekly newsletter Bits and Paradoxeswhere I share various contents I encounter in my life — essays, podcasts, videos, books, discussion threads, ideas, poetry, music, and other fascinating things.

I tend to be more “contemplative” on topics touching the universe, life, and existence. So, you might encounter some existential crises scattered here and there in bits… It’s a way for me to reinforce my mental models and knowledge to connect the dots at best.
